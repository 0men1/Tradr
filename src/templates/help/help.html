Welcome to Tradr

What is this?

This is a place for all of your stock news needs! It will help you learn more about what news articles are saying about certain stocks.


Why was it made?

This was made to allow everyone the privilege of knowledge and have the same resources as other competitive traders. My inspiration was from YahooFinance. Whenever I would go to that website to read about what journalists have to say about certain stocks I always end up clicking off thinking this isnt the information I need. However, here you are able to get full descriptions/summaries of every article on yahoo finance to make your life easier.


How to use it?

In order to use this tool follow these steps:

1. Go to the home page

2. Type in the stock ticker you want to search for

3. Click the search button

4. Read through the summaires to figure out which article stand out to you

5. Click on the article you want to read

6. You will be redirected to Yahoo Finance and you can read the full article there

This was made with the help of AI and machine learning. The AI will read through the articles and summarize them for you. This is a great tool to use if you are in a rush and want to get the most information in the least amount of time.
